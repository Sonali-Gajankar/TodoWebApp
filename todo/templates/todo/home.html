Welcome to The Synch!


An app to keep track of all of your tasks for the day, week, month!
Often times people find it difficult to organize and manage their days. Given that we are merely human beings we can add to this the natural phenomenon of forgetfullness!
Together all these factors result in tasks getting compromised or altogether being eliminated. All in all this adds up to stressful days.


Well we are here to help you manage some of these tangible stressors in your life!
Even though this is a fairly simple tool it will help you organize your day to day activities and plan your days out in a more effective manner.
The application provides a simple yet user friendly interface to keep track of all of your tasks. We do hope you will enjoy using this app!
